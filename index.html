WHO ARE WE?

Green Choice Mara is a community-based organization located in Narok town.
We majorly target schools and institutions to educate children about planting trees and conserving the environment.
We aim to leave every place we visit greener and educate people about climate change adaptation and mitigation.
We also focus on empowering women and even the girl child by providing free sanitary towels to the girls. We also insist on the importance of completion of education to ensure that every child, both boys and girls are able to reach their maximum potential and mould a future for themselves.
